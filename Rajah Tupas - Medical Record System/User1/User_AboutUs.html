Mission

Rajah Tupas is a Philippine-based Healthcare, particularly in Cebu City, with the vision to bring healthcare of international standards to this region, to evolve as a premier Medical Service Center in the country and to touch the lives of the people we serve through excellence in clinical care, quality and commitment.

Vision

A community in which all people achieve their full potential for health and well-being across the lifespan. We work to be trusted by patients, a valued partner in the community, and creators of positive change.

Values

Our Approach to Health Care:

1. We believe that providing high quality, accessible health care is our reason for being. We seek to be a model for other community health centers.

2. We continuously examine the services we provide and what is needed in the community. We look for cracks in the health care system, fill those, and move to fill new ones. When other community resources develop to address those needs, we make intentional decisions to apply our resources differently.

Work Environment

1. We create a dynamic, forward-moving, innovative organization.

2. We recognize and appreciate the contributions and accomplishments of individuals and teams.

3. We seek and reward suggestions and innovation.

4. We have a strong work ethic, yet we don’t stifle our individual personalities.
